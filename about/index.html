About Me

Hi my name is Leon and I’m currently studying computer science at the Christian-Albrechts-University in Kiel, Germany. I have a strong preference for coding in Python and I am mainly interested in data analysis and prototyping.

Since my early childhood I am fascinated by computers and how they work. So it comes to no surprise that my best friend and me got to ‘modify’ video games when we were still in fifth grade. Some of those mods are still around today because they are just pretty cool.

It would probaly describe myself as a ‘Backend-Developer’, because everything Frontend related doesn’t really catch my attention (even though I know about plain HMTL/CSS/JS). That said I have a variety of skills in:

  • Backend development using Python and Flask (sometimes Django)
  • Databases (mainly relational)
  • Basic skills in server and network administration
